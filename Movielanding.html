Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

2001 • PG • 152 min • Family, Fantasy

Plot Summary

On his eleventh birthday, Harry Potter, a young boy living under the stairs, discovers he is the orphaned son of two powerful wizards and possesses magical powers of his own. He is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he begins his incredible journey into a world of magic, friendships, and hidden dangers, leading to a confrontation with the dark wizard who murdered his parents.
